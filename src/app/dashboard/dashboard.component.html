Welcome to AIT Collab

AIT Collab is a platform to connect students in AIT. Our main aim to foster a fruitful and collaborative relationship between a junior and a senior student. As a new student it might be overwhelming to figure out and navigate around the campus. We offer to provide a medium to connect with seniors from your department and participate in events created by your mentor. Apart from that, you can also connect to other students from your department and more.

If you are a senior student, you can opt in the mentor program where junior student can request you to connect with you. You can opt in the mentor program by updating your profile Information in your profile page.
